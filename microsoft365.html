Configured and secured, not just licensed

Your Microsoft 365 tenant managed properly. Teams structured. SharePoint organised. Exchange protected.

Buying Microsoft 365 licenses is easy. Making them work for your business is another matter. Default settings leave security holes. Teams sprawls into chaos. SharePoint becomes a dumping ground. Exchange exposes data it should protect.

We manage Microsoft 365 the way it should be managed. Properly configured from day one. Actively maintained as your business changes. Security policies that protect without getting in the way. And when something breaks or someone has a question, they call us directly.

What We Do

Ongoing management, not just setup

Microsoft 365 needs active attention. We provide it.

Teams governance

Team creation policies, naming conventions, guest access controls, channel structure. Keeps collaboration organised instead of chaotic.

SharePoint organisation

Site architecture, permission inheritance, document libraries that make sense. Your files findable, your data controlled.

Exchange management

Mailbox policies, shared mailboxes, distribution groups, mail flow rules. Email working the way your business needs it to.

Security configuration

Conditional access, MFA enforcement, data loss prevention, sensitivity labels. Protection that actually protects.

User lifecycle

New starters provisioned correctly. Leavers offboarded securely. Licenses reallocated, not wasted.

License optimisation

Right licenses for each role. Regular reviews as your team changes. No overpaying for features nobody uses.

Microsoft sells you licenses. They provide documentation and support forums. When you call their helpdesk, you wait. When you finally get through, they read from scripts.

We know your environment. We understand your business. When your finance team cannot access a shared mailbox at 4pm on a Friday, we fix it in minutes because we already know your setup. That is the difference between a license vendor and a managed service partner.
