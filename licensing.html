Never miss a renewal again

Software licensing management that saves you money and keeps your business compliant.

Software licensing has become unnecessarily complicated. Between Microsoft's constant plan changes, security vendor bundles, and the endless stream of SaaS subscriptions, most businesses have no clear picture of what they're actually paying for.

We've seen organisations paying for licenses assigned to people who left years ago. Subscriptions that auto-renewed at higher prices without anyone noticing. Security tools that expired, leaving gaps nobody knew about until something went wrong.

Our approach is simple: we bring transparency to your software spend. We track every license, monitor every renewal date, and make sure you're paying for what you actually use. When renewals approach, we review your usage, get competitive quotes, and present options with enough time for you to make informed decisions.

The result is straightforward pricing with no surprises, automated alerts well before anything expires, right-sized licenses that match actual usage, and one consolidated invoice instead of dozens scattered across different vendors.

Microsoft 365

M365 licensing that makes sense

Microsoft 365 licensing is complicated. With dozens of plans, add-ons, and bundles, it's easy to overpay for features you'll never use or miss out on capabilities that could transform how your team works.

We've been managing Microsoft licenses since the days of Office 365 subscriptions. We know which plans actually deliver value, which add-ons are worth the money, and how to structure licensing to save you thousands over time.

More importantly, we stay on top of Microsoft's constant changes. New features, retired products, price adjustments, we track it all so you don't have to.

Network Security

FortiGate and security licensing

Your firewall is only as good as its subscriptions. FortiGate devices need active licenses for threat intelligence updates, web filtering, application control, and support. When these lapse, your protection degrades quietly, often without anyone realising until it's too late.

What we manage

We handle FortiCare support contracts that cover hardware replacement, firmware updates, and technical support. FortiGuard services provide the threat intelligence, web filtering, and application control updates that keep your protection current. For organisations needing comprehensive coverage, we manage UTM and Enterprise bundles that combine all protection features into a single subscription.

Bundle selection

The UTM bundle covers most SMB needs with application control, intrusion prevention, web filtering, and antivirus. Enterprise bundles add advanced malware protection and sandbox analysis for organisations handling sensitive data. The 360 Protection bundle provides maximum coverage including FortiCloud Premium and 24/7 support for businesses that can't afford any gaps.

We also handle FortiCloud subscriptions for central management, logging, and analytics, plus hardware lifecycle planning so you know when devices are approaching end-of-life and need replacement.

Proactive Management

How we handle renewals

Missed renewals cause disruptions. Last-minute renewals cost more. The solution is proactive management that keeps you informed and prepared well before any deadline.

We track every renewal date across your software portfolio. Ninety days out, we start the renewal process: reviewing usage, getting competitive quotes, and presenting options. You make informed decisions with time to spare, not rushed choices under pressure.

For multi-year agreements, we analyse the total cost of ownership and help you decide whether locking in rates makes financial sense for your business. Sometimes it does, sometimes it doesn't, and we'll tell you honestly which applies to your situation.

When renewals complete, you get one consolidated invoice covering all your software. Simpler accounting, easier budget tracking, and a clear record of exactly what you're paying for.
