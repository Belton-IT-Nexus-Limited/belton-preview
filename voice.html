Business VoIP

Modern phone systems that travel with your team. One number, any device, no missed calls.

The desk phone had a good run. But when half your team works remotely and the other half is between meetings, a phone that only rings on a desk is a problem.

Cloud voice changes the equation. Your business number follows your team - to their mobile, laptop, or home office. Calls route intelligently so the right person answers. And because it runs in the cloud, there's no hardware room to maintain or expensive PBX to replace.

Same professional presence. Complete flexibility.

What modern voice looks like

Work from anywhere

Take calls on your mobile app, laptop, or desk phone. Your business number stays professional - callers never see personal numbers. Perfect for hybrid teams, field staff, and anyone who doesn't sit at the same desk every day.

Intelligent call routing

Auto attendants greet callers and direct them to the right team. Ring groups ensure someone always picks up. After-hours rules handle calls automatically. No more callers listening to endless rings.

Voicemail that works for you

Messages arrive in your inbox with transcriptions so you can read or listen. Decide quickly what needs a callback now versus tomorrow. Forward important voicemails to colleagues when you're away.

Predictable costs

Unlimited NZ and AU calling for one monthly price per user. No per-minute charges, no surprise bills, no accountant asking why the phone bill doubled last month.

Teams calling: Voice where you already work

If your team lives in Microsoft Teams for chat and meetings, Teams Phone is the logical next step. PSTN calling directly inside the same app - make and receive calls without switching tools.

Your business number shows on outbound calls. Voicemail, auto attendant, and call queues are all built in. When a colleague is on a call, Teams knows. Transfer calls as easily as forwarding a message.

For organisations deep in the Microsoft ecosystem, this unifies communications without adding another platform to manage.

Unified communications: More than just calls

Voice is just one channel. Modern business communication spans calls, video, chat, and presence - knowing who's available and how to reach them. The best solutions bring these together.

We deploy systems where a missed call can become a voicemail, then a Teams message, then a callback reminder - all without manual intervention. Where your receptionist sees colleague availability before transferring. Where after-hours calls can route to on-call staff or an answering service.

The goal isn't more technology. It's fewer missed connections and faster responses.

Migrating from a legacy PBX? We handle the transition - porting numbers, configuring call flows, and training your team. Most businesses are fully operational on day one. Let's talk about your setup.
