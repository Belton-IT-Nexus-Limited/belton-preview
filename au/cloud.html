Infrastructure that scales with you

Your systems in world-class data centres. Accessible from anywhere. Ready when you are.

The server room used to be the heart of every business. Now it's often just a liability. Hardware ages, capacity runs out, and one bad weekend can mean days of recovery. Cloud changes that equation entirely.

We help Australian businesses move their infrastructure to Azure, AWS, or hybrid environments that match how they actually work. Not a wholesale rip-and-replace, but a considered approach that puts the right workloads in the right places. Some things belong in the cloud. Some things work better on-site. The trick is knowing which is which.

Whether you're running a single file server or a complex multi-site operation, we design cloud solutions that fit your business today and scale for where you're headed.

What we deliver

Cloud means different things to different organisations. Here's what we actually do.

Azure and AWS infrastructure

Servers, storage, and applications running in Microsoft or Amazon data centres. Pay for what you use, scale when you need to, and let someone else worry about hardware failures.

Hybrid environments

Sometimes you need both. Local servers talking to cloud services, with secure connections between them. We design setups where on-premise and cloud work together seamlessly.

Virtual desktops

Full Windows desktops running in the cloud. Your team logs in from any device and gets their familiar environment with all their applications. Perfect for remote teams or BYOD policies.

Cloud migrations

Moving from physical servers or outdated infrastructure to cloud platforms. We handle the planning, the migration, and the cleanup. Most of our migrations happen with zero user downtime.

Ongoing management

Cloud environments need attention. We monitor, optimise, patch, and respond to issues. Active cost management means you're not paying for resources you don't need.

Azure or AWS?

Both are excellent. The right choice depends on your situation.

Microsoft Azure

If you're already using Microsoft 365, Azure is the natural extension. Deep integration with Entra ID, seamless authentication, and a familiar management experience.

Azure makes particular sense for organisations standardised on Microsoft technologies, those needing hybrid identity with on-premise Active Directory, or teams already comfortable with the Microsoft ecosystem.

Amazon Web Services

AWS has the broadest range of services and often wins on raw compute economics. If you're running Linux workloads, containerised applications, or need specific capabilities, AWS might be the better fit.

We work with both platforms and can help you choose based on your actual requirements rather than marketing. Sometimes the answer is a mix of both.

Our approach to migrations

Every migration starts with understanding what you have and where you want to be. We audit your current environment, map dependencies, and identify what should move, what should stay, and what should be retired entirely.

We plan migrations in phases, minimising risk and disruption. Critical systems get extra attention. We run parallel environments when needed, so you always have a fallback. And we don't declare victory until everything is running smoothly and your team is comfortable.

Post-migration, we stick around. Cloud environments need ongoing optimisation. We monitor performance, right-size resources, and actively manage costs so you're not surprised by your monthly bill.
