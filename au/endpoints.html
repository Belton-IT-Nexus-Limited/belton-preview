Your fleet, handled

Every laptop, desktop, and phone. Monitored, patched, protected, and supported.

Slow laptops. Update prompts during presentations. Antivirus alerts that nobody knows how to handle. These distractions add up to lost hours and frustrated staff.

A well-managed device doesn't spring surprises. It gets patched overnight, monitored continuously, and fixed before problems become visible. That takes dedicated attention across every machine in your business.

We take that off your plate. Every device monitored, updated, and protected automatically. Problems resolved before anyone notices. And when something does come up, we handle it quickly so your team stays productive.

How it works

Always watching, quietly fixing

Every managed device runs monitoring software that tracks health around the clock. CPU, memory, disk, network, security status. When something goes outside normal parameters, we know immediately.

Most issues resolve automatically. A runaway process gets killed. A stuck service restarts. Disk space gets cleaned. For problems needing human attention, we're on it before anyone complains.

Updates deploy on your schedule. Security patches go out quickly because they're urgent. Feature updates get tested first and roll out after hours. No more restart prompts interrupting important work.

Lifecycle

From purchase to retirement

Device management isn't just monitoring. It's the whole lifecycle. When you need new hardware, we help you choose what actually fits your needs, not the flashiest specs. Devices arrive pre-configured so your team is productive immediately.

We track device age and performance. When it's time to refresh, you'll know ahead of time. Planned replacements instead of emergency purchases.

End-of-life devices get wiped securely and disposed of responsibly. Your data stays protected throughout.

Built on Microsoft Intune and enterprise-grade tools. The same technology large organisations use, configured for your environment. Integrates with Microsoft 365 and your existing infrastructure.
