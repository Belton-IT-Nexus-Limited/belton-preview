The Process

What happens next

We don't believe in complicated sales processes. When you reach out, we'll start with a conversation about your current setup, what's working, what isn't, and where you want to be. This helps us understand if we're a good fit for each other. No pressure, no hard sell.

If it makes sense to continue, we'll review your environment in more detail. This might be a quick chat with your current IT person, a look at your systems, or a security assessment. We'll tell you exactly what we need and why.

From there, we put together a clear proposal with exactly what we recommend, why, and what it costs. No hidden fees, no surprises. If something doesn't make sense, ask. We'd rather have an honest conversation than a confused client.

Once you're ready to proceed, we'll schedule onboarding. Most clients are fully transitioned within two to four weeks, depending on complexity. We handle the heavy lifting while you keep running your business.

What to Expect

Working with us

We're straightforward about how we operate. Here's what you can expect once you're a Belton client.

Direct access to real engineers. No ticket queues, no call centres, no waiting on hold.
Proactive communication. We'll tell you about problems before they affect you, and we'll recommend improvements when we see opportunities.
Fixed monthly pricing. Your bill is predictable. Support calls don't cost extra.
Honest advice. We'll tell you what you need to hear, not what you want to hear. Sometimes that means recommending against spending money.
Regular reviews. We'll meet with you periodically to discuss what's working, what could be better, and what's coming next.
Good Fit

Who we work best with

We're not the right fit for everyone, and that's okay. We work best with organisations that value long-term relationships, want a partner rather than just a vendor, and understand that good IT is an investment, not just an expense.

Our sweet spot is businesses between 20 and 500 people. Big enough to need proper IT infrastructure, small enough that you want a team who knows your name. Professional services firms, manufacturers, not-for-profits, and growing companies tend to be our best fit.

If you're looking for the absolute cheapest option, we're probably not it. If you want IT that actually works, handled by people who care, that's what we do.
